This document establishes the concept of a protocol in which a person's digital behavioural trail — accumulated over time and cryptographically verified — becomes an access key to specialised platforms, without exposing personal data to third parties. Concept authored by Nadzim Huseinau, June 1, 2026.
01

The Problem

Modern digital platforms rely either on self-declaration (forms, resumes, profiles) or on behavioural data — but collected centrally, without the user's awareness, serving the platform rather than the individual.

This produces three systemic failures:

  • Behavioural data belongs to the platform, not the person.
  • Access to specialised communities is gated by self-description, which is trivially falsifiable.
  • Valuable closed environments (for idea generators, introverts, rare specialists) either do not exist or lack a reliable entry filter.
02

Core Principle

A metatype is defined not by what a person does, but by the statistical stability of patterns in the space of consequences of their actions, verified across time horizons from 30 to 540 days.

The distinction is fundamental:

What is NOT collectedWhat is collected instead
"Opened an article about finance"Changed behaviour 3 days after an informational contact
"Sent a message at 2am"Chronotype of activity over 90 days — a stable pattern
"Visited an introvert platform"Depth vs. breadth of social ties over 6 months
"Bought a physics book"Vector of intellectual drift — direction and velocity

Behaviour can be simulated in the short term. Consequence patterns over 6–18 months are significantly harder to fake.

ZK-ProofZero-Knowledge Proof: the user proves metatype membership without revealing underlying data.
Blockchain (L2)Stores only the vector hash and proof. Immutable timestamp. Platforms receive an access token via smart contract.
DAO GovernanceDecentralised organisation governs model parameters, prevents monopolisation and algorithmic drift.
04

Metatype as an Asset

A metatype is not a static label — it is a dynamic multi-dimensional vector formed from several signal classes:

  • Chronobiological pattern — stable time zones of activity
  • Cognitive style — depth vs. breadth of information processing
  • Social topology — density, depth, and speed of relationship formation
  • Intellectual drift vector — direction and velocity of shifting interests
  • Response to uncertainty — behavioural pattern in the absence of a clear answer

The profile appreciates over time: the longer and richer the history, the more accurate the metatype, and the more specialised the platforms that become accessible. This creates a built-in incentive to accumulate data.

05

Example Platforms

Idea Bounty

Entry: metatype "systemic generator" (top 5% by associative breadth and conceptualisation speed). An idea is timestamped on-chain. If implemented by anyone within N years — automatic payout from the pool, regardless of the author's involvement in execution.

Platform for introverts

Entry: metatype "deep solitary" (low breadth, high depth of connections, specific chronotype). Compatibility is determined by overlap of behavioural vectors, not questionnaires. People do not describe themselves — the system sees them through their behaviour.

07

Prior Art & Status

Existing projects cover individual aspects only:

  • Sovrin, Polygon ID — self-sovereign identity, without behavioural vectors
  • Gitcoin Passport, Orange Protocol — on-chain reputation, without psychographic metatypes
  • Crystal Knows, Humantic AI — behavioural analytics, without user data ownership
  • World ID (Worldcoin) — proof of personhood: "are you human?" but not "what kind of human are you?"
The combination of local agent + consequence trail + ZK-proof of metatype + platforms gated by metatype + user ownership of profile + DAO governance — has not been implemented by any existing project as of this writing.
